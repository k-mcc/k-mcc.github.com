About

I currently study computer science and environmental sciences at the University of Virginia. Last year, in an atmospheric chemistry lab at UVA, I developed a prototype for an air pollution injustice map of Houston, Texas. My research project focused on making satellite observations of air pollution more accessible. Before that, I interned at IBSS, where I developed a customer feedback system integrated with Salesforce. In my free time, I love exploring new places, taking photos, and going on hikes. While on campus, I write for the humor section of the Cavalier Daily, UVA's student newspaper, and I'm in an improv comedy club.
